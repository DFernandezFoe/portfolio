Who am I?

My name is Daniel Fernandez, a Madrid based UI/UX designer. I find innovative web-design solutions for users and businesses.

BloomBox

Bloombox was one the first projects I tackled. As a flower delivery service looking to enhance it's online presence, the project was all about following UX research techniques in order to arrive at design decisions.

MediC

While this project seemed like a simple task (It's an alarm app!); the seemingly simple is often difficult to approach. There were many design considerations that would have been overlooked without UX research; many of which went beyond the scope of a "simple alarm."

ParcelPidgeon

This was a personal project of mine, one which sprung up from my interest and previous work in supply chain logistics. Using Amazon, UPS and DHL as case studies, I set out to identify and solve UX/UI issues currently widespread across parcel delivery systems and websites.
